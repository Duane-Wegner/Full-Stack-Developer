The Power of Type: My CSS Typography Journey

Exploring the Art of Digital Letterforms Through freeCodeCamp

By: Dev Duane

When I first started learning CSS, typography seemed like a background detail—important, but secondary to layout and color. That changed when I reached the typography section of the freeCodeCamp curriculum. Suddenly, I saw how much the feel of a website could shift with the right choice of font, size, spacing, and alignment.

I learned to respect the rhythm of line-height, the subtlety of letter-spacing, and the elegance of font pairing. Choosing between serif and sans-serif became a creative decision rather than a default. Through countless tweaks and trials, I’ve come to see typography not just as styling—but as storytelling. Thanks to freeCodeCamp, I now approach CSS with a designer’s eye and a developer’s precision.
